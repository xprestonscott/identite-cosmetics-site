The Artist Behind Identité

Corey James

Identité Cosmetics & Skincare Studio was founded by Oklahoma City artist Corey James — a licensed, certified, and insured permanent cosmetics and corrective aesthetics specialist.

The studio's name says it all: identity. Every brow, lip, and treatment plan is built around the person in the chair, not a one-size-fits-all template.

Licensed

Practicing under full state licensure for permanent cosmetics and aesthetic treatments.

Certified

Trained and certified across multiple permanent makeup and skincare modalities.

Insured

Fully insured studio operations for total client peace of mind.

The Studio

Treated as a composition, not a procedure.

Identité approaches permanent cosmetics the way a portrait artist approaches a canvas — with a real consultation first, custom color development for every client, and an outcome that's tailored to the individual features in front of him, not a fixed style applied the same way to everyone.

That same philosophy carries into the studio's corrective skincare work: chemical peels, advanced resurfacing, and nano infusion treatments designed around what a client's skin actually needs, with results that build over a realistic timeline rather than overnight promises.

Identité is located on NW 56th Street in Oklahoma City and serves clients from across the OKC metro — including Edmond, Yukon, Mustang, Bethany, and Warr Acres.
